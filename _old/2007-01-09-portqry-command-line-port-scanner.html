PortQryV2.exe is a command-line utility that you can use to help troubleshoot TCP/IP connectivity issues. Portqry.exe runs on Windows 2000-based computers.The utility reports the port status of TCP and UDP ports on a computer you choose. For details, see Knowledge Base Article Knowledge Base Article 310099.
